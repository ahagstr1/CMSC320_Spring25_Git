Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Answer: Git is good for version control, collaboration, backup, branching 
and merging, and it is widely used so there's a lot of documentation.

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.

Answer: Data science can be applied to management consulting to help 
businesses make data-driven decisions, optimize operations, and improve 
performance. For example, a consulting firm working with a retail company can
use data science to analyze customer purchasing patterns. By leveraging ML
models and predictive analysis, they can identify trends and segment customers
to give the retail company specific steps to implement personalized marketing
strategies.
